## Purpose

Ensure every completed roof meets company quality standards, manufacturer specifications, and customer expectations before final closeout.

## Scope

All roofing installation and repair jobs before customer sign-off and final invoicing.

## Inspection Levels

| Level | When | Who | Documentation |
|-------|------|-----|---------------|
| In-Process | During installation | Crew Lead | Daily log |
| Crew Completion | Job complete | Crew Lead | Completion form |
| Final QC | Before closeout | QC Inspector/PM | QC checklist |
| Customer Walk | With customer | PM/Sales | Sign-off |

## Procedure

### 1. Crew Completion Check (By Crew Lead)

Before crew leaves job site:

**1.1 Roof Surface Inspection**
- [ ] All shingles installed and aligned
- [ ] Consistent exposure throughout
- [ ] No exposed nails or fasteners
- [ ] No damaged or defective shingles
- [ ] Shingles laying flat, no lifting
- [ ] No debris on roof surface

**1.2 Ridge and Hip Check**
- [ ] Ridge cap properly aligned
- [ ] Ridge cap nails not exposed
- [ ] Hip shingles aligned and secure
- [ ] Ridge vent installed correctly (if applicable)
- [ ] Ends properly terminated

**1.3 Flashing Inspection**
- [ ] Pipe boots properly installed
- [ ] Step flashing at all roof/wall junctions
- [ ] Counter flashing where required
- [ ] Valley flashing/shingles correct
- [ ] Chimney flashing complete and sealed

**1.4 Edge Treatment**
- [ ] Drip edge on all eaves
- [ ] Drip edge on all rakes
- [ ] Starter strip visible at eave
- [ ] Clean, straight edge line

**1.5 Cleanup**
- [ ] Magnetic nail sweep complete
- [ ] Debris removed from all areas
- [ ] Landscaping cleared
- [ ] Driveway swept

### 5. Quality Standards Reference

**Acceptable vs. Unacceptable:**

**Shingle Alignment:**
- Acceptable: Within 1/8" of chalk line
- Unacceptable: Visible waviness or misalignment

**Exposure:**
- Acceptable: Consistent per manufacturer spec (+/- 1/8")
- Unacceptable: Visible variation or short/long exposure

**Nail Placement:**
- Acceptable: In manufacturer's nail zone
- Unacceptable: High nails, exposed nails, overdriven

**Flashing:**
- Acceptable: Sealed, no gaps, properly integrated
- Unacceptable: Gaps, loose, improperly overlapped

**Ridge Cap:**
- Acceptable: Centered, aligned, nails not visible
- Unacceptable: Off-center, crooked, exposed nails

### 6. Customer Walk-Through

**6.1 Schedule After QC Pass**
- Contact customer to schedule
- Allow 30-45 minutes
- Have documentation ready

**6.2 Walk-Through Process**
1. Start with overall view from street
2. Point out quality features
3. Walk perimeter of home
4. Explain work completed
5. Show key areas (valleys, flashings)
6. Address any questions
7. Review warranty information

**6.3 Customer Sign-Off**

```
□ I have received warranty information

Company Representative: ________ Date: _____
```

### 7. Failed Inspection Protocol

**7.1 If Inspection Fails:**
1. Complete punch list
2. Notify operations manager
3. Schedule correction crew
4. Notify customer of delay (if needed)
5. Re-inspect after corrections
6. Document for quality tracking

**7.2 Recurring Issues**
Track patterns:
- Same crew repeated issues
- Same type of defect
- Training needs identified

### 8. Quality Metrics

Track monthly:
- First-time pass rate (target: 90%+)
- Common punch list items
- Callback rate
- Customer satisfaction scores

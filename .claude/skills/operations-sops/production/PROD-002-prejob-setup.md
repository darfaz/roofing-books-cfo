## Purpose

Ensure all jobs are properly prepared before crews arrive to maximize efficiency and prevent delays.

## Scope

All roofing installation and major repair jobs.

## Pre-Job Timeline

| Days Before | Task | Owner |
|-------------|------|-------|
| 7 | Confirm schedule, review job file | Ops Manager |
| 5 | Order materials, schedule delivery | Ops Manager |
| 3 | Confirm customer, prep job packet | Office |
| 2 | Material delivery | Supplier |
| 1 | Final weather check, dumpster delivery | Ops Manager |
| 0 | Job start | Crew Lead |

## Procedure

### 1. Job File Review (7 Days Before)

**1.1 Document Verification**
- [ ] Signed contract on file
- [ ] Deposit received and cleared
- [ ] Measurements verified
- [ ] Material list complete
- [ ] Color confirmed with customer
- [ ] Permit obtained (if required)
- [ ] HOA approval (if required)
- [ ] Insurance claim approved (if applicable)

**1.2 Special Requirements Check**
- [ ] Access issues identified
- [ ] Utility locate needed?
- [ ] Specialty materials needed?
- [ ] Subcontractors needed (gutters, skylights)?
- [ ] Any customer special requests?
- [ ] Safety hazards identified?

### 2. Material Ordering (5 Days Before)

**2.1 Order Preparation**
Generate material order from estimate:
- Verify quantities against measurements
- Add standard overage percentages:
  - Shingles: 5-10%
  - Underlayment: 10%
  - Accessories: 5%
- Include all job-specific items
- Note color and manufacturer

### 3. Customer Preparation (3 Days Before)

**3.1 Customer Reminder Call**
```
"Hi [Name], [Your Name] from [Company]. Just
confirming your roof project starts [Day].

Quick reminders:
- Materials arrive [day before/day of]
- Crew arrives between [time range]
- Please move cars from driveway
- Any fragile wall items should come down

Any questions before the big day?"
```

**3.2 Customer Prep Email**
```
Subject: Your Roof Project Starts [Day]!

Hi [Name],

Excited to get started on your roof in [X] days!

PRE-JOB CHECKLIST - Please complete before we arrive:

VEHICLES:
□ Move all cars from driveway
□ Clear street parking in front of house

INTERIOR:
□ Remove fragile items from walls (the vibration
  from work can shake things loose)
□ Cover items in attic with sheets
□ Note: You may hear loud noises - this is normal!

EXTERIOR:
□ Move patio furniture away from house
□ Cover or move plants near foundation
□ Remove any items leaning against house
□ Take down any hanging decorations

PETS:
□ Plan for pets to stay inside or away during work
□ Gate any areas where pets might escape

ACCESS:
□ Leave gate unlocked or provide code: [if applicable]
□ No need to be home - just leave access available

OTHER:
□ Alert neighbors about upcoming work
□ Postpone any landscape services this week

Material delivery is scheduled for [Date].
Crew arrives [Date] at approximately [Time].

See you soon!

[Signature]
```

**5.1 Delivery Confirmation**
Day before delivery, confirm with supplier:
- Delivery time
- Complete order
- Truck access to property

**5.2 Delivery Supervision**

- Verify materials match order
- Check for damaged materials
- Direct placement location
- Sign delivery receipt

### 6. Dumpster Coordination

### 7. Final Day-Before Checklist

**7.1 Operations Manager Checklist**
By 4:00 PM day before:
- [ ] Weather check - GO or POSTPONE
- [ ] Materials delivered and verified
- [ ] Dumpster delivered
- [ ] Crew confirmed and briefed
- [ ] Job packet delivered to crew lead
- [ ] Customer notified of crew arrival time
- [ ] Any specialty equipment confirmed

**7.2 Crew Lead Notification**
Send crew lead:
- Job packet
- Start time and address
- Any last-minute changes
- Weather update

**7.3 Customer Notification**
```
Text: "Hi [Name]! Confirming your roof project starts
tomorrow. [Crew Lead Name] and team will arrive
between [Time range]. Weather looks good! Any
questions, call me at [Phone]. - [Your Name]"
```

### 8. Contingency Planning

**8.1 Weather Delay Protocol**
If weather forces postponement:
1. Decide by 4:00 PM day before (or 6:00 AM morning of)
2. Notify customer immediately
3. Notify crew immediately
4. Contact material supplier (hold delivery)
5. Contact dumpster company
6. Reschedule at first available date

**8.2 Material Shortage Protocol**
If materials are short or damaged:
1. Document issue with photos
2. Contact supplier immediately
3. Assess if job can start without items
4. If critical, delay start
5. If minor, proceed with rush order

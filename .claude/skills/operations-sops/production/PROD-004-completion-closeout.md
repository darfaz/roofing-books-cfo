## Purpose

Ensure every job is properly completed, inspected, documented, and closed out to meet quality standards and customer expectations.

## Scope

All roofing installations from final day of work through payment collection.

## Completion Day Procedure

### 1. Final Installation Tasks

**1.1 Punch List Inspection (by Crew Lead)**
Before cleanup begins:
- [ ] Ridge cap fully installed and aligned
- [ ] All flashings complete and sealed
- [ ] Pipe boots properly installed
- [ ] Drip edge complete, no gaps
- [ ] All penetrations sealed
- [ ] No exposed nails or fasteners
- [ ] Shingles laying flat, no lifting
- [ ] Valleys properly cut and sealed
- [ ] No debris on roof surface

**1.2 Address Any Issues Immediately**
Do not leave punch list items:
- Fix before cleanup
- Document any items requiring return trip
- Note materials needed

### 2. Final Cleanup

**2.1 Roof Cleanup**
- [ ] Remove all loose debris
- [ ] Remove all equipment
- [ ] Clear gutters of debris
- [ ] Final visual inspection from ground

**2.2 Ground Cleanup**
- [ ] Magnetic nail sweep - entire property
  - Front yard
  - Back yard
  - Driveway
  - Street in front
  - Neighboring yards (permission first)
- [ ] Sweep driveway and walkways
- [ ] Remove all debris from landscaping
- [ ] Remove tarp and protection materials
- [ ] Check flower beds and mulch areas
- [ ] Inspect fence lines

**2.3 Property Inspection**
- [ ] Check for any property damage
- [ ] Document pre-existing issues
- [ ] Note any concerns for customer

**2.4 Material Management**
- [ ] Count remaining materials
- [ ] Load excess for return
- [ ] Leave proper amount for warranty repairs
- [ ] Document what remains with customer

### 3. Customer Walk-Through

**3.1 Request Walk-Through**
```
"Hi [Name], we've finished up your roof. It looks
great! Do you have a few minutes to walk around
with me? I'd love to show you what we did and
answer any questions."
```

**3.2 Walk-Through Points**
Show and explain:
1. **Overview** - Stand back and view entire roof
2. **Key features** - Valleys, ridge vent, flashings
3. **Problem areas resolved** - Compare to before
4. **Cleanup** - Point out thoroughness
5. **Leftover materials** - Explain what's left

**3.3 Address Questions**
Answer all questions thoroughly:
- Warranty information
- Maintenance expectations
- Normal settling/sealing process
- Who to call with issues

**3.4 If Customer Not Home**
- Leave completion notice on door
- Take photos of completed work
- Call/text customer immediately
- Schedule walk-through if desired

### 4. Photo Documentation

**Required Completion Photos:**
- [ ] Each roof plane (4+ photos)
- [ ] Ridge line detail
- [ ] Valley detail
- [ ] Flashing at walls
- [ ] All pipe penetrations
- [ ] Gutters/drip edge
- [ ] Full front view of home
- [ ] Full rear view of home
- [ ] Close-up of shingle pattern
- [ ] Any special features

**Photo Quality Standards:**
- Clear, in focus
- Good lighting
- Include context
- No shadows on detail shots

### 5. Paperwork Completion

**5.1 On-Site Forms**
Complete before leaving:
- [ ] Daily job log (final day)
- [ ] Material usage log
- [ ] Quality inspection checklist
- [ ] Completion certificate (signed if possible)
- [ ] Customer satisfaction survey (if used)

### 6. Equipment Removal

**6.1 End-of-Job Equipment Check**
- [ ] All ladders loaded
- [ ] All tools collected
- [ ] Safety equipment accounted for
- [ ] Leftover materials loaded
- [ ] Nothing left on property

**6.2 Dumpster Pickup Coordination**
- Call for pickup before leaving site
- Confirm pickup date with company
- Inform customer of expected pickup
- Check for HOA time restrictions

### 7. Office Notification

**7.1 Job Completion Report**
Call or text to office:
- Job complete
- Final hours worked
- Any issues/callbacks needed
- Customer satisfaction status
- Any damage incidents
- Change order totals (if any)

**7.2 Upload Required Items**
Same day if possible:
- All completion photos
- Signed documents
- Change order documentation
- Material receipts

### 8. Post-Completion Process

**8.1 Final Invoice Preparation**
Office prepares within 24 hours:
- Original contract amount
- Approved change orders (+/-)
- Less deposit paid
- Final balance due

**8.2 Invoice Delivery**
```
Subject: Your Roof is Complete - Final Invoice

Hi [Name],

Congratulations on your beautiful new roof!

Attached is your final invoice. As a reminder:

Contract Amount: $[Amount]
Change Orders: $[Amount]
Deposit Paid: ($[Amount])
─────────────────────────
Balance Due: $[Amount]

Payment is due upon completion. We accept:
• Check
• Credit Card
• ACH Transfer
• Financing (if applicable)

IMPORTANT DOCUMENTS ATTACHED:
• Final Invoice
• Completion Certificate
• Warranty Information
• Manufacturer Registration Instructions

Please let me know if you have any questions!

[Signature]
```

**8.3 Payment Collection**
- Standard terms: Due upon completion
- Follow-up Day 3 if not received
- Follow-up Day 7 with phone call
- See OFFICE-004 for collection process

### 9. Warranty Registration

**9.1 Workmanship Warranty**
Company provides:
- Standard: [X] years
- Prepare warranty document
- Send to customer with invoice

**9.2 Manufacturer Warranty Registration**
Guide customer or register for them:
- Online registration process
- Information needed:
  - Homeowner name
  - Property address
  - Installation date
  - Contractor information
  - Product codes installed

### 10. Quality Follow-Up

**10.1 48-Hour Check-In**
Phone call or text:
```
"Hi [Name], [Your Name] from [Company]. Just
checking in to make sure you're happy with your
new roof. Any questions or concerns since we
finished up? We want to make sure everything
exceeds your expectations!"
```

**10.2 Review Request**
If customer is satisfied:
```
"That's wonderful to hear! If you have a moment,
would you mind sharing your experience in a
Google review? It really helps our small business.
I can text you the link."
```

### 11. Job File Closeout

**11.1 File Completion Checklist**
Ensure job file contains:
- [ ] Signed contract
- [ ] Change orders (if any)
- [ ] Before photos
- [ ] After photos
- [ ] Material receipts
- [ ] Permit (if required)
- [ ] Inspection results (if required)
- [ ] Completion certificate
- [ ] Warranty documents
- [ ] Payment records
- [ ] Customer communications

**11.2 Financial Closeout**
- Invoice marked paid
- Job costing completed
- Profitability calculated
- Commissions processed

**11.3 Archive Job**
- Move to completed jobs folder
- Maintain for [X] years minimum
- Accessible for warranty claims

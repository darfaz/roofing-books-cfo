## Purpose

Ensure every lead receives prompt, professional response to maximize conversion and customer experience. Speed to lead is the #1 factor in closing rates.

## Scope

Applies to all inbound leads from any source: phone, web form, referral, door knock, or marketing campaign.

## Performance Standards

| Metric | Target | Minimum Acceptable |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| Phone Answer Rate | 95% | 85% |
| Voicemail Return | 5 minutes | 15 minutes |
| Web Form Response | 5 minutes | 30 minutes |
| Appointment Set Rate | 70% | 50% |

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Receptionist/CSR | Answer calls, log leads, schedule appointments |
| Sales Rep | Return calls, respond to web leads, conduct appointments |
| Sales Manager | Monitor response times, coach team, handle escalations |

## Procedure

### 1. Phone Leads (Inbound Calls)

**1.1 Answer Protocol**
```
Ring 1-2: Answer with greeting
"Good [morning/afternoon], [Company Name], this is [Name],
how can I help you today?"
```

**1.2 Qualification Questions**
Ask in natural conversation:
- [ ] "What can we help you with today?" (Determine need)
- [ ] "What's the address of the property?" (Service area check)
- [ ] "Is this for a home you own?" (Ownership)
- [ ] "What prompted your call today?" (Urgency/trigger)
- [ ] "Have you noticed any interior damage like water spots?" (Severity)

**1.3 Appointment Setting**
```
"I'd love to have one of our roof specialists come take a look.
They can give you an honest assessment and answer all your questions.
We have availability [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] -
which works better for you?"
```

**1.4 Information Collection**
Required fields:
- [ ] Full name
- [ ] Property address
- [ ] Phone number (primary + backup)
- [ ] Email address
- [ ] Best time to reach
- [ ] How they heard about us
- [ ] Brief description of need

**1.5 Confirmation**
```
"Great, you're all set for [Day, Date] at [Time].
[Rep Name] will be there. You'll receive a confirmation
email/text shortly. Is there anything else I can help with?"
```

### 2. Missed Calls / Voicemails

**2.1 Response Time**: Return within 5 minutes during business hours

**2.2 Return Call Script**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. I'm returning
your call about your roof. I apologize I missed you - do you
have a moment to chat?"
```

**2.3 If No Answer**
- Leave voicemail (see script below)
- Send text message immediately after
- Log in CRM with follow-up task

**2.4 Voicemail Script**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company] returning your call.
I'd love to help you with your roofing needs. You can reach me
directly at [Phone]. I'll also send you a quick text.
Looking forward to speaking with you!"
```

**2.5 Follow-Up Text**
```
Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. Just left you a
voicemail. Happy to help with your roof - what time works best
to connect? - [Your Name]
```

### 3. Web Form Leads

**3.1 Response Time**: Within 5 minutes during business hours

**3.2 Response Sequence**
1. **Immediate** (within 5 min): Phone call attempt
2. **If no answer**: Voicemail + text + email
3. **+1 hour**: Second call attempt
4. **+4 hours**: Third call attempt
5. **+24 hours**: Continue per follow-up SOP

**3.3 Initial Email Response**
```
Subject: Your Roof Inspection Request - [Company Name]

Hi [First Name],

Thank you for reaching out about your roof! I just tried
calling you at [Phone] and will try again shortly.

I'd love to schedule a free inspection at your convenience.
What day and time works best this week?

In the meantime, here's a bit about what to expect:
• We'll thoroughly inspect your entire roof system
• You'll get an honest assessment - no pressure
• If repairs or replacement are needed, we'll provide options

You can reach me directly at [Phone] or just reply to this email.

Looking forward to helping you!

[Signature]
```

### 4. Referral Leads

**4.1 Response Time**: Same day, within 2 hours

**4.2 Referral Call Script**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company]. [Referrer Name]
mentioned you might need some help with your roof and suggested
I give you a call. They're one of our favorite customers!
What's going on with your roof?"
```

**4.3 Referrer Acknowledgment**
Send thank-you text/call to referrer within 24 hours:
```
"Hey [Referrer Name]! Just wanted to let you know I connected
with [Lead Name] - thank you so much for the referral!
We really appreciate you thinking of us."
```

### 5. Storm/Emergency Leads

**5.1 Priority Handling**
- Move to front of queue
- Express empathy for situation
- Emphasize urgency of inspection

**5.2 Storm Lead Script**
```
"I'm so sorry to hear about the storm damage. That must be
stressful. Let me get one of our specialists out there quickly
to assess the situation and make sure your home is protected.
Are you available [today/tomorrow]?"
```

### 6. Lead Logging Requirements

Every lead must be logged in CRM with:
- [ ] Contact information
- [ ] Lead source
- [ ] Conversation notes
- [ ] Appointment (if set) or reason not set
- [ ] Follow-up tasks scheduled
- [ ] Assigned sales rep

### 7. Escalation Protocol

Escalate to Sales Manager when:
- Lead mentions attorney or litigation
- Lead is hostile or threatening
- Lead requests owner specifically
- Commercial opportunity over $50,000
- Lead mentions competitor by name
- Insurance claim with complexity

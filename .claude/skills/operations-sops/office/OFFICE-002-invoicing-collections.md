## Purpose

Ensure timely, accurate invoicing and effective collection of payment for all completed work while maintaining positive customer relationships.

## Scope

All invoicing and collection activities for residential and commercial roofing work.

## Payment Terms

**Standard Payment Structure:**
- Deposit: [X]% at contract signing
- Balance: Due upon completion

**Alternative Structures (by approval):**
- 1/3 - 1/3 - 1/3 (Deposit, Mid-project, Completion)
- Insurance: ACV at start, RCV supplement at completion
- Financing: Per financing partner terms

## Procedure

### 1. Deposit Collection

**1.1 At Contract Signing**
Sales rep collects:
- Deposit amount per contract
- Payment method (check/card/ACH)
- Complete payment information

**1.2 Accepted Payment Methods**
- [ ] Check (payable to [Company Name])
- [ ] Credit card (Visa, MC, Amex, Discover)
- [ ] ACH/Bank transfer
- [ ] Financing (through [Partner])
- [ ] Cash (receipt required)

**1.3 Deposit Processing**
- Process within 24 hours
- Record in accounting system
- Note payment method on job file
- Send receipt to customer

### 3. Payment Processing

**3.1 Check Payments**
- Deposit within 24 hours
- Record in accounting system
- Note check number

**3.2 Credit Card Payments**
- Process immediately
- Record confirmation number
- Email receipt to customer

**3.3 ACH Payments**
- Verify receipt in 2-3 business days
- Record transaction ID
- Email confirmation to customer

**3.4 Financing Payments**
- Verify funding with finance partner
- Record funding amount and date
- Any customer responsibility follows standard collection

### 4. Payment Tracking

**4.1 Accounts Receivable Log**
Track all outstanding invoices:

| Invoice # | Customer | Amount | Due Date | Status | Last Contact |
|-----------|----------|--------|----------|--------|--------------|
| | | | | | |

**4.2 Status Categories**
- Current (0-7 days)
- Past Due (8-30 days)
- Delinquent (31-60 days)
- Collections (60+ days)

### 5. Collection Process

**5.1 Standard Collection Timeline**

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 0 | Invoice sent |
| 3 | Reminder email (if not paid) |
| 7 | Phone call + email |
| 14 | Second phone call + letter |
| 21 | Final notice call + certified letter |
| 30 | Manager involvement |
| 45 | Collections escalation |
| 60+ | Collections agency or legal |

**5.2 Day 3 - Gentle Reminder Email**
```
Subject: Friendly Reminder - Invoice [#]

Hi [First Name],

Just a quick reminder that your invoice for [Amount]
is due. I wanted to make sure you received it!

If you've already sent payment, thank you - please
disregard this note.

Payment options:
[List payment methods]

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Thanks!
[Signature]
```

**5.3 Day 7 - Phone Call + Email**
```
Call Script:
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company].
I'm calling about the invoice we sent on [Date]
for your roof project. I wanted to check if you
received it and if you had any questions about
the balance of [Amount]?

[If not yet paid]
"When would be a good time for us to expect payment?"

[If issue with work]
"I'm sorry to hear that. Let me connect you with
someone who can help resolve this right away."
```

Email (same day if no answer):
```
Subject: Following Up - Invoice [#]

Hi [First Name],

I tried reaching you by phone today regarding
invoice [#] for $[Amount] from your roof project.

I want to make sure there are no issues preventing
payment. If you have questions or concerns about
the work, please let me know right away so we can
address them.

If everything is good, you can pay easily at [Link]
or by calling [Phone].

Please give me a call when you have a moment.

[Signature]
```

**5.4 Day 14 - Second Call + Letter**

Mailed letter:
```
[Date]

[Customer Name]
[Address]

RE: Past Due Invoice #[Number]
    Property: [Address]
    Amount Due: $[Amount]
    Original Due Date: [Date]

Dear [Name],

Your account is now [X] days past due. We have
attempted to reach you by phone and email without
success.

Please remit payment of $[Amount] within 7 days
to avoid further collection activity.

Payment can be made by:
• Check mailed to: [Address]
• Credit card: Call [Phone]
• Online: [URL]

If there is an issue with your project that is
preventing payment, please contact me immediately
at [Phone] so we can resolve it.

We value your business and want to maintain our
positive relationship.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
[Company]
```

**5.5 Day 21 - Final Notice**
```
FINAL NOTICE - ACCOUNT PAST DUE

[Date]

[Customer Name]
[Address]

RE: PAST DUE - Invoice #[Number]
    Amount Due: $[Amount]
    Days Past Due: [X]

Dear [Name],

Despite multiple attempts to contact you, your
account remains unpaid. This is your final notice
before we escalate collection efforts.

PAYMENT OF $[AMOUNT] IS DUE IMMEDIATELY.

If we do not receive payment or hear from you
within 10 days, we will have no choice but to:
• Place your account with a collection agency
• Report to credit bureaus
• Pursue legal remedies
• File a mechanic's lien on the property

We do not want to take these steps. Please
contact us immediately at [Phone] to resolve
this matter.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
```

**5.6 Manager Escalation (Day 30)**
- Review account with management
- Consider:
  - Payment plan option
  - Negotiated settlement
  - Lien filing
  - Collection agency
  - Legal action

### 6. Payment Plans

**6.1 When to Offer**
- Customer experiencing genuine hardship
- Account 15+ days past due
- Customer communicating
- Good faith effort to pay

**6.2 Payment Plan Terms**
Maximum: 3 monthly payments
Minimum payment: $[X] or 33% of balance
Documentation required: Signed agreement

**6.3 Payment Plan Agreement**
```
PAYMENT PLAN AGREEMENT

Date: _______________

Customer: _______________________________

• Missed payment voids this agreement
• Full balance becomes immediately due
• Collection activity will resume

Customer Signature: _____________ Date: _____
Company Representative: _________ Date: _____
```

### 7. Mechanic's Lien Process

**7.1 When to File**
- Account 45+ days past due
- No payment plan in place
- No response to collection efforts
- Amount justifies filing cost

**7.2 Lien Requirements**
[Varies by state - customize for your state]
- Preliminary notice requirements
- Filing deadline from completion
- Proper property description
- Recording requirements

**Consult attorney before filing liens**

### 8. Reporting

**8.1 Weekly AR Report**
Review with management:
- Total AR by aging bucket
- Individual accounts past due
- Collection activities completed
- Issues requiring escalation

**8.2 Monthly Metrics**
- Average days to payment
- Collection rate percentage
- Bad debt write-offs
- Lien filings

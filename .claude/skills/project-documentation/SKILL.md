### OSHA Compliance Reminders

**Fall Protection (29 CFR 1926.501)**
- Required at 6 feet or greater on residential
- Options: Guardrails, Safety Nets, Personal Fall Arrest
- Anchor points must support 5,000 lbs per worker

**Ladder Safety (29 CFR 1926.1053)**
- Extend 3 feet above landing
- 4:1 ratio (1 foot out for every 4 feet up)
- Secure at top and bottom
- Face ladder when climbing

**Housekeeping (29 CFR 1926.25)**
- Keep walkways clear
- Dispose of debris promptly
- Cover or guard floor holes

                    [COMPANY LOGO]

                    [License #]

Homeowner: [Name]
Property: [Address]

Warranty Period: [X] Years from installation

COVERAGE
--------
This warranty covers defects in workmanship related
to the installation of roofing materials at the above
property, including:

• Improper installation of shingles
• Improper installation of flashings
• Improper installation of ventilation
• Leaks resulting from installation errors

EXCLUSIONS
----------
This warranty does not cover:
• Damage from severe weather events
• Damage from falling objects
• Damage from improper maintenance
• Normal wear and aging of materials
• Damage from alterations by others
• Manufacturer material defects (see manufacturer warranty)
• Acts of God

WARRANTY TRANSFER
-----------------

one time during the warranty period. Transfer
must be registered within 30 days of property sale.

## Data Sources for Tracking

### Primary Sources
- **Roofing Contractor Magazine**: roofingcontractor.com/articles (PE news, acquisitions)
- **LinkedIn**: Company announcements, exec posts
- **Press Releases**: Platform company websites
- **Local Business Journals**: Regional deal announcements

### Secondary Sources
- **PitchBook/CapIQ**: Institutional deal data (requires subscription)
- **BizBuySell**: Listed businesses, asking prices
- **Private equity firm websites**: Portfolio company announcements

## Key Metrics to Track

### Platform Health Indicators
- Number of add-on acquisitions per year
- Geographic expansion patterns
- Integration success (retention of acquired companies)
- Organic vs. acquired revenue growth

### Market Trends
- New platform formations
- PE fund exits
- Strategic buyer activity (manufacturers, distributors)
- Public company acquisitions

## Notes

- Deal values are rarely disclosed for private transactions
- Multiples are estimated based on industry benchmarks unless confirmed
- "Platform" = initial PE investment creating a new consolidation vehicle
- "Add-on" = acquisition by an existing PE-backed platform

# Roofing Customer Communications

Create professional, effective customer communications that build trust and drive action while maintaining a friendly, approachable tone.

## When to Use This Skill

- Drafting emails to customers (estimates, updates, follow-ups)
- Writing SMS/text messages
- Creating appointment confirmations and reminders
- Sending project status updates
- Requesting reviews and referrals
- Handling complaints or concerns
- Storm damage outreach

## Communication Principles

### Tone Guidelines
- **Professional** but not stiff
- **Friendly** but not overly casual
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Helpful** always
- **Urgent** when appropriate (storm damage)
- **Grateful** for their business

### Key Messaging Points
1. We protect your home and family
2. We stand behind our work
3. We communicate clearly and often
4. We respect your time and property
5. We're local and here for the long term

## Best Practices

1. **Respond quickly** - Same-day response for all inquiries
2. **Use their name** - Personalization builds connection
3. **Be specific** - Dates, times, amounts, names
4. **Call to action** - Tell them what to do next
5. **Follow up** - Don't let leads go cold
6. **Proofread** - Typos hurt credibility

THE PROFIT FLOOR FRAMEWORK

Before you price another job, you need to know your break-even number:

1

Calculate Your True Monthly Overhead

Add up everything that gets paid whether you roof or not: rent, insurance, office staff, truck payments, software, your salary.

2

Add Your Labor Burden

Take your hourly labor rate and add 25-35% for workers comp, payroll taxes, unemployment. A $25/hr worker costs $31-34/hr.

3

Find Your Break-Even Revenue

Divide monthly overhead by gross profit margin. If overhead is $40K and GP is 35%, you need $114K/month to break even.

4

Build the Guardrail Into Every Bid

Now every estimate includes overhead recovery. You know before you bid whether it'll make money.

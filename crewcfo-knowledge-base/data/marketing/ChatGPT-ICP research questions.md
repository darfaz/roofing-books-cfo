Processes that exist on paper but are not followed in reality

Turning approved processes into guided flows and checklists

Tracking whether steps were actually followed and by whom

Ingest & map: Connect Spec-OS to your existing repositories (Drive, Notion, Confluence, etc.) and upload SOPs, policies, and process docs. The system parses and maps them into workflows.

Normalize & improve: Spec-OS groups related documents, identifies overlaps/gaps, and helps you standardize each workflow into a clear, versioned “Spec” (who does what, when, and with what tools).

Activate runbooks: Each Spec becomes a live runbook you can launch as:

Step-by-step checklists with owners and due dates

Decision trees with branching logic

Triggers and automations (notifications, updates in other tools)
with human approval gates wherever needed.

Measure & improve: Spec-OS tracks adherence (what steps were skipped, where work gets stuck), and suggests improvements or automation candidates over time.

Agencies / professional services firms with complex workflows

Traditional knowledge bases (Confluence, Notion) store information but do not enforce or guide execution.
